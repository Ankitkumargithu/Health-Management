About Karshipla Healthcare

Karshipla is a government-recognized export house with over 40 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. We offer more than 500 formulations across various therapeutic segments including Oncology, Cardiac, Orthopedic, Diabetic, and many more.

  • Government recognized Star Export House
  • WHO-GMP certified manufacturing facilities
  • Comprehensive regulatory and documentation support
  • End-to-end supply chain management
