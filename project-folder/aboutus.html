Founder's Journey Since 1982

1982
Began a career with Salgaukars Goa – Bevit Pharmaceuticals as a Medical Representative. Covered the regions of Belgaum, Bijapur, Karwar, and Dharwad.
1984
Promoted to Area Manager based in Hubballi. Successfully expanded operations to Bellary, Davangere, Raichur, and Gulbarga.
1987
Promoted to Regional Manager in Bangalore. Recruited and led a team of 8 Medical Representatives... Expanded operations to Mysore and South Canara, growing the team to 25 medical representatives. Secured the #1 performance ranking in Karnataka within just two years of launch.
1988
Entrusted with expansion into Maharashtra.
1990
Launched operations in Andhra Pradesh with 20 MRs and 5 managers... Joined Meyer Organics Pvt Ltd as Regional Manager, overseeing South India.
1994
Promoted to Zonal Manager, successfully expanding and managing the entire Southern region until 1998.
1998
Appointed to lead expansion in Northwest India, launching operations in Gujarat..., Rajasthan, and Punjab with a team of 80. Later promoted to Deputy Sales Manager, overseeing market entry across North and Northeast India with a team of 95.
1995
Promoted to National Sales Manager based in Mumbai..., leading nationwide operations and strategic growth.
2000
Successfully launched two specialty divisions: Exel and Orthopedic.
